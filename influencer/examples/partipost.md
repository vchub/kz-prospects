# Partipost (Singapore) - Business and Tech Service Summary

**Checked date:** March 4, 2026

## What Partipost is (business model)

- APAC-focused crowd influencer marketing platform.
- Connects brands/agencies with large pools of nano/micro creators ("Partiposters").
- Supports both self-serve campaign setup and managed execution support.
- Revenue model is campaign-budget based with platform fees.

## What Partipost provides (tech/service layer)

1. Campaign Manager platform for brands

- Create campaign briefs
- Define participation model (registration vs direct)
- Set payouts and budget
- Review and approve creator submissions
- Track campaign performance and export reports

1. Creator workflow/app

- Creators join eligible campaigns
- Submit deliverables
- Track payout/wallet status

1. Task and attribution approach

- Includes traffic tasks (e.g., link-sharing flows)
- Public materials indicate strong campaign workflow tooling
- Public evidence of deep merchant-stack integrations (e.g., native Shopify + GA4-level attribution) is limited

## Positioning insight

- Strong at bottom-up scaling via micro/nano creator volume and standardized campaign operations.
- Less evidence (publicly) that it is a deep commerce-infra/attribution platform compared with full-stack partnership platforms.

Partipost is a crowdsourced influencer marketing platform designed to connect brands with "everyday people" and nano-influencers for performance-driven campaigns. Its primary service is enabling brands to scale word-of-mouth marketing by activating thousands of smaller creators simultaneously.

### Core Service Model

The platform operates on a "bottom-up" scaling model that prioritizes authenticity and engagement over traditional celebrity reach:

- **Influencer Participation:** Any social media user with as few as **200 followers** can browse the Partipost mobile app, select campaigns they love, and participate instantly.

- **Brand Workflow:** Brands use a centralized **Campaign Manager dashboard** to launch campaigns, set specific KPIs, and manage the entire workflow—from briefing to content collection, review, and approval.

- **Multi-Channel Support:** The service primarily supports activations across **TikTok, Instagram, and Facebook**.

### Key Features and Capabilities

- **Mass Scalability:** By lowering the entry barrier for creators, the platform allows brands to activate "creative squads" or thousands of voices at once, which is often more affordable than hiring a single macro-influencer.

- **Performance Focus:** The service is built to achieve specific marketing objectives, leveraging the high trust and credibility that nano-influencers have within their immediate social circles.

- **Self-Serve Interface:** It offers a streamlined, self-serve interface for brands to deploy campaigns quickly without the need for manual outreach or long-term relationship management.

### Business and Monetization Model

- **Pricing:** Partipost typically utilizes a **pay-per-post** or fixed-price campaign model, making it highly accessible for Small and Medium Enterprises (SMEs) with controlled budgets.

- **Geographic Reach:** Headquartered in Singapore, the service has expanded across Southeast Asia and the APAC region, including **Indonesia, Malaysia, Taiwan, and the Philippines**.

In summary, Partipost's service functions as a high-velocity marketplace that automates the coordination of micro-level influence, turning everyday consumers into brand advocates at scale.
