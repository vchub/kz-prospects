# Subproject: Live Social Shopping

## One-line concept
Build a creator-led live commerce platform where hosts sell products in real time and users can purchase with minimal friction during the stream.

## What this is
Live Social Shopping combines:
- Live video (real-time product demo + Q&A)
- Social interaction (chat, reactions, trust, FOMO)
- Instant commerce (in-stream checkout or 1-2 tap purchase)

## Why this subproject
This format has shown large-scale traction in markets where content, payments, and fulfillment are tightly integrated. For our platform strategy, it is a high-potential extension because it links creator influence directly to measurable transactions.

## Core value proposition
- For brands: faster conversion loops, high-intent traffic, measurable performance by host/session/product.
- For creators: stronger monetization than static posts (fixed fee + performance + recurring audience trust).
- For users: richer buying confidence via live demo, questions, and limited-time offers.

## What success looks like (concept-level)
- Repeatable live sessions with consistent conversion behavior.
- Reliable attribution from live interaction to purchase.
- Strong creator and brand retention due to transparent outcomes and payouts.
- Sustainable economics without excessive subsidy dependence.

## Non-goals for now
- Not a full implementation plan.
- Not a broad creator discovery product.
- Not a generic social app; commerce outcomes are primary.
